Rc Car Arduino, Serres.

For this project, I attempted to remotely control an RC car using WiFi or Bluetooth through the installation of an Arduino Uno R3. However, my efforts were unsuccessful due to either incompatible hardware or wiring issues. Despite this setback, I was able to make the car operational through Ethernet. Ultimately, I was able to program the car to move forward or backward and included ultrasonic sensors to detect obstacles and stop the car when an object was within 20 cm of the RC car.
